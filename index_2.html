Know more about us

Established in 2020, Mountain Warriors is a passionate trekking group that has been exploring the breathtaking landscapes of the Western Ghats for over three years. Our journey began with a thrilling trek to Ankai Fort, and since then, we have embarked on numerous unforgettable adventures, conquering more than 20 majestic forts, including Ahiwant, Harihar, Ravlya Javlya, Kanchan Manchan, Dhodap, and many others.

At Mountain Warriors, we believe that every mountain has a story to tell, and our mission is to uncover these tales while fostering a deep connection with nature. Our experienced and skilled team of trek leaders is dedicated to providing safe and exhilarating trekking experiences for adventurers of all levels.

We pride ourselves on promoting responsible trekking practices, respecting the environment, and supporting local communities during our expeditions. Our treks not only offer an escape from the daily hustle but also present an opportunity to forge lasting friendships and create memories that will stay etched in your heart forever.

Whether you are a seasoned trekker seeking new challenges or a first-time explorer eager to take your first steps into the wilderness, Mountain Warriors welcomes you to join us on an extraordinary journey of self-discovery, camaraderie, and a profound appreciation for the wonders of nature.
