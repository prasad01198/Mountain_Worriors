About Us

Established in 2020, Mountain Warriors is an adventurous trekking group with over three years of thrilling expeditions. We've conquered 20+ forts, including Ankai, Ahiwant, Harihar, Ravlya Javlya, Kanchan Manchan, and Dhodap. Our mission is to uncover nature's stories and promote responsible trekking practices while forging lasting bonds and creating unforgettable memories. Join us for an extraordinary journey of self-discovery and camaraderie in the mesmerizing Western Ghats!
